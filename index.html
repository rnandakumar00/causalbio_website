How it works
01

Program review

We review your trial data, biomarker readouts, and clinical context to identify mechanistically plausible failure modes.

02

Model calibration

Pathway models are calibrated against literature-derived constraints across multiple biological contexts.

03

Retrospective analysis

We simulate across genotypic and phenotypic contexts to identify where the biology diverged from the drug's intended mechanism.

04

Prospective prediction

For active programs: resistance hypotheses, combination response, and patient stratification with mechanistic justification.
